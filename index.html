Why This Coin

FAQ

Why this coin?

The market rarely rewards comfort. SCX stands for The Second Chance: acting while the signal is still quiet, after you already know what hesitation costs. The Instagram clip is part of why the project exists. It is the same emotional note, shared out loud.

What makes it different?

Most coins sell a fantasy exit. SCX sells a mirror: timing, accountability, and moving before the crowd needs your conviction to feel safe. No outcome is promised. The name is the thesis.

Why is Bitcoin on this page?

As one familiar picture of hindsight regret, not as the point of the brand. The main story is The Second Chance and the clip. Bitcoin shows up briefly as a cultural example of missing something big while busy with forgettable noise. SCX is not “the next Bitcoin” and nothing here compares returns.

How to buy, step by step

  1. Get Phantom (Solana wallet). Go to phantom.app and install the browser extension or mobile app. Create a new wallet, write down your recovery phrase offline, and never share it with anyone.
  2. Add SOL for gas and the swap. Buy SOL on an exchange you trust, then withdraw to your Phantom Solana address. You need enough SOL for the purchase plus a small amount left over for network fees (fees are usually low, but keep a little extra).
  3. Open Pump.fun. Visit pump.fun in the same browser where Phantom is installed. Click Connect wallet and approve the connection in the Phantom popup.
  4. Open the SCX coin page. Use the Open Pump.fun link in the Pump.fun section above once it points to your live coin, or use Pump’s search and paste the contract address you copied from this page so you don’t buy the wrong token.
  5. Swap SOL for SCX. Enter how much SOL you want to spend, review the quote and slippage if shown, then confirm the transaction in Phantom. Wait for the Solana network to confirm. Your SCX balance will appear in Phantom under the token list (you may need to add the token manually using the contract address if it does not show up right away).
