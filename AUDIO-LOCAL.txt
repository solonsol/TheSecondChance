Background audio (local + YouTube Short)
=========================================

The file scx-background.webm is the audio track from:
  https://www.youtube.com/shorts/aOhNqSnPv5Y

Regenerate it (e.g. after you replace the Short) from the project folder:

  .\tools\yt-dlp.exe -f ba -o "scx-background.%(ext)s" --no-playlist --force-overwrites "https://www.youtube.com/shorts/aOhNqSnPv5Y"

That produces scx-background.webm (no ffmpeg required). The page also lists
scx-background.mp3 as an optional second <source> if you export MP3 yourself
for Safari / older browsers.

Deployed sites use only these first-party audio files (no YouTube iframe on the page).
